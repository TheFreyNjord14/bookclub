About Us

Formed in 1995 after the Bellevue College rebranded to Bellevue University, the Bellevue University Book Club has been a cornerstone of literary discussion in the community.

We meet monthly to discuss a wide range of books, from classic literature to contemporary bestsellers. Our mission is to foster a love of reading and create a welcoming space for book lovers to connect and share their thoughts.

Our founders John Doe and Jane Smith started the club with the vision of creating a community where people could come together to discuss their favorite books and discover new ones. Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant community of readers who share a passion for literature.
